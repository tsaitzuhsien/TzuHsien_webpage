I am a graduate student advised by Professor Pangfeng Liu. I am a member of the Parallel and Distributed Computing Lab at National Taiwan University. I received my Bachelor of Science degree in the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University.

I am interested in designing and analyzing algorithms that address real-world problems. My master's thesis focuses on accelerating physical simulations on graphics processing units (GPUs). Prior to my master's thesis, I worked on reducing computation time and memory usage in deep neural network training. For more details, please refer to my publications.

This webpage contains my personal information, curriculum vitae, some interesting questions, my answers to them, and experiments involving the basic of computing. Please sit back, relax, and enjoy!

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me via email.
